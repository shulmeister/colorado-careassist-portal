## Articles

### 1. America's Seniors Are Overmedicated
- **Source:** Wall Street Journal
- **URL:** https://www.wsj.com/health/healthcare/americas-seniors-are-overmedicated-f397bb1d?st=oNStuY&reflink=desktopwebshare_permalink
- **Summary:** Important insights about medication management for older adults.
- **Relevance:** Both audiences - medication safety is crucial for patients and providers

### 2. Solving the Home Care Quandary
- **Source:** New York Times
- **URL:** https://www.nytimes.com/2025/12/02/health/seniors-aging-home-care.html
- **Summary:** Exploring challenges and solutions in home care services for seniors.
- **Relevance:** Both audiences - home care challenges affect everyone in the ecosystem

### 3. Sage advice on aging and dying from a Colorado geriatrician
- **Source:** Colorado Public Radio
- **URL:** https://www.cpr.org/2025/11/17/colorado-geriatrician-on-aging-dying-nursing-homes/
- **Summary:** Dr. Erika Altneu, a geriatrician in Salida, shares insights on aging, medications, loneliness, and end-of-life care.
- **Relevance:** 
  - **Clients:** Very relevant - practical advice about aging, medications, and end-of-life planning
  - **Referral Sources:** Relevant - geriatric care insights, medication management, care transitions

### 4. Winter weather poses risks for those living with Alzheimer's and dementia
- **Source:** Cleveland.com
- **URL:** https://www.cleveland.com/opinion/2025/12/winter-weather-poses-risks-for-those-living-with-alzheimers-and-dementia-mary-ertle.html
- **Summary:** Safety tips for caregivers during winter months, including prevention strategies for wandering and cold weather hazards.
- **Relevance:** 
  - **Clients:** Highly relevant - practical winter safety tips for families
  - **Referral Sources:** Relevant - safety concerns they should be aware of for referred patients
