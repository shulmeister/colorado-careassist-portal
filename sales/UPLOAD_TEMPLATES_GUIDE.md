# How to Upload Newsletter Templates to Brevo

Your newsletter templates are ready to upload to Brevo's saved templates. Follow these steps:

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### 1. Open Brevo
Go to https://app.brevo.com and log in.

### 2. Navigate to Templates
- Click **Marketing** in the left sidebar
- Click **Campaigns**
- Click **Templates** (or go directly to Email Templates)

### 3. Create a New Template
- Click **Create a new template** (or the + button)
- Select **Start from scratch**
- Choose **HTML custom code** (or "Paste your code")

### 4. Upload the HTML
- Open one of your template files in a text editor:
  - `newsletter_january_2025_clients.html` - For clients
  - `newsletter_january_2025_referral_sources.html` - For referral sources
- Select ALL the HTML content (Cmd+A / Ctrl+A)
- Copy it (Cmd+C / Ctrl+C)
- Paste into Brevo's HTML editor (Cmd+V / Ctrl+V)

### 5. Save the Template
- Click **Save & Activate** (or **Save**)
- Give it a name like:
  - "Client Newsletter Template - January 2025"
  - "Referral Source Newsletter Template - January 2025"
- Click **Save**

### 6. Repeat for Other Templates
Repeat steps 3-5 for any other templates you want to upload.

## Template Files Available

1. **newsletter_january_2025_clients.html** - Ready-to-send client newsletter
2. **newsletter_january_2025_referral_sources.html** - Ready-to-send referral sources newsletter
3. **newsletter_template_clients.html** - Base template for clients (reusable)
4. **newsletter_template_referral_sources.html** - Base template for referral sources (reusable)

## Using Templates After Upload

Once uploaded, your templates will appear in Brevo's "Saved Templates" list when creating campaigns, just like your Mailchimp imports.

You can then:
- Use them directly in Brevo campaigns
- Edit them in Brevo's editor
- Duplicate and modify for future newsletters
