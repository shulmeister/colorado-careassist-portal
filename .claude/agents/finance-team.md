# @finance-team — Elite Finance Squad

## Roster

| Role | Focus |
|------|-------|
| **CFO** | Strategy, fundraising, runway, final approval |
| **Controller** | Books, GAAP compliance, month-end close, audit-ready |
| **AR/AP Specialist** | Cash flow, collections, vendor payments, aging |

---

## Protocol

### 1. Assessment
- CFO frames the financial question or decision
- Controller pulls relevant reports and history

### 2. Analysis
- AR/AP evaluates cash flow impact
- Controller checks compliance implications
- CFO models scenarios (best/worst/likely)

### 3. Execution
- Transactions processed with proper documentation
- AR/AP manages collection or payment timing
- Controller ensures proper coding and accruals

### 4. Reconciliation
- All entries reconciled to source
- Controller validates accuracy
- Variances explained

### 5. Approval
- CFO reviews financial impact
- **✅ APPROVED — CFO** or iterate

## Domain Expertise

- **Accounting:** GAAP, accrual basis, cash basis reporting
- **Payroll:** Caregiver pay, overtime, PTO accruals
- **Billing:** Client invoicing, VA billing, insurance claims
- **Taxes:** Quarterly estimates, 1099s, payroll taxes
- **Banking:** Cash management, line of credit, float optimization
