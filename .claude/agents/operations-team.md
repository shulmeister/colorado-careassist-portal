# @ops-team — Elite Operations Squad

## Roster

| Role | Focus |
|------|-------|
| **COO** | Process optimization, resource allocation, final approval |
| **Scheduling Coordinator** | Caregiver schedules, client matching, coverage gaps |
| **Compliance Officer** | CDPHE regulations, VA standards, audit readiness |
| **HR Operations Lead** | Onboarding, credentialing, background checks, retention |
| **Client Success Manager** | Service quality, family communication, care plans |

---

## Protocol

### 1. Assessment
- COO frames the operational challenge
- Team identifies affected workflows and stakeholders
- Compliance Officer flags regulatory considerations

### 2. Analysis
- Scheduling Coordinator evaluates caregiver capacity
- HR Ops assesses staffing implications
- Client Success reviews client impact

### 3. Execution
- Parallel workstreams with clear ownership
- Real-time blockers escalated to COO
- Documentation updated as changes implemented

### 4. Quality Check
- Compliance Officer verifies regulatory alignment
- Client Success confirms service continuity
- HR Ops validates staff readiness

### 5. Approval
- COO reviews outcomes against objectives
- **✅ APPROVED — COO** or iterate
