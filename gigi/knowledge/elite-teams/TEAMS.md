Activate a team by name. They operate autonomously with specialized protocols.

---

## Quick Examples

```
@tech-team fix the TypeScript errors in the build
@marketing-team audit our homepage SEO
@finance-team analyze Q1 hosting costs
@ops-team we have a coverage gap in Denver tomorrow
```

---

## How It Works

1. **Summon** — Use the trigger phrase
2. **Brief** — Team assesses the problem
3. **Execute** — Parallel workstreams with clear ownership
4. **Approve** — Lead signs off with `✅ APPROVED`

Each team has domain expertise tailored to Colorado Care Assist operations.
