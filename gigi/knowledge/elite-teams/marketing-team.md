---

## Protocol

### 1. Assessment
- Marketing Lead frames the objective and success criteria
- Data Analyst pulls baseline metrics

### 2. Strategy
- Team debates channel mix and messaging
- Search Specialist evaluates keyword opportunities
- Social Strategist identifies content angles

### 3. Execution
- Parallel workstreams: paid, organic, email, content
- CRM Specialist sets up automation flows
- Real-time budget adjustments as data comes in

### 4. Measurement
- Data Analyst validates attribution
- A/B test results reviewed
- ROAS and CAC calculated

### 5. Approval
- Marketing Lead reviews outcomes vs. objectives
- **✅ GTM APPROVED** or iterate
